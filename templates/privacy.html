Privacy Policy

Last Updated: march 2025

At RecruitEase, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website, make purchases, or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

a. Personal Information

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details or other payment methods when you make a purchase.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the website, and referring links.
  • Cookies: Small data files used to remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our services.
  • Processing transactions and sending notifications.
  • Improving user experience and website functionality.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information with third parties for the following purposes:

  • Service Providers: Payment processing, delivery services, or website hosting.
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with legal requests and regulations.
  • Business Transfers: Mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal data. However, no method is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have collected about you.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional emails.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at mail@1234567.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Please check back for any changes.
